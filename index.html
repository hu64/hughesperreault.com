About

Hi! I am Hughes, a computer vision enthusiast specialized in object recognition and general scene understanding in urban environments. I am particularly interested in using computer vision for greater safety and automation in transportation applications. In recent years, I have worked primarily with deep learning, including tools like TensorFlow and PyTorch, even though I still have a strong interest in classical computer vision. During my Ph.D., I produced results on multiple popular road user datasets including Cityscapes, KITTI, India Driving, UA-DETRAC and UAVDT. I am currently working as an AI scientist at Thales where I work on general optimization, multimodal image registration and fusion, and object detection and tracking among other things.
